Bio

Taylor Mason is a percussionist and multi-disciplinary musician currently studying at the University of Oklahoma, where he performs with the Pride of Oklahoma Marching Band, the OU Percussion Studio, and various concert, orchestral, and opera ensembles. With experience marching Drum Corps International including seasons with the Colt Cadets (2023), Genesis Drum & Bugle Corps (2024), and Spirit of Atlanta (2026), Taylor brings a high level of performance discipline, rhythmic precision, and musical versatility to every ensemble. He is also active as a private lesson instructor in the Norman/Oklahoma City Metro area, teaching students across percussion, keyboard fundamentals, and beginner piano.
