My Story

I’m a University of Oklahoma student building my path in both computer engineering and music. I love the structure and problem-solving of engineering, and I bring that same discipline into percussion—especially through ensemble performance, teaching, and DCI experience.

This site is a single place to explore my engineering projects (code, systems thinking, and technical growth) and my music work (performances, recordings, and teaching).
